**Project Proposal**

What is your website about?

My website will be a personal website that highlights my travels and my artworks.

What is the objective of your website? (why is it important)

Hopefully, this website will be an opportunity for me to share my experiences and some photos and drawings from these places. Young adults who are interested in traveling, photography and art.
My target audience will also be people interested in learning about me.

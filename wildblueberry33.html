About Me

I chose to study at Babson College because it has a great business program and I like the emphasis on entrepreneurship here. Apart from the business courses offered at Babson, I was also drawn to the opportunities to study abroad. When I visited Babson, my tourguide had talked about his experience on the BRIC program. Although I did a different abroad program, I am really grateful for that experience. I am currently a senior at Babson with concentrations in Economics and Finance. After graduation, I hope to get a full time job in an industry that is challenging and interesting to me. My plans for post-college are not set in stone yet as I am still in the recruitment process.

My Interests

In my free time, I love to travel, golf, and draw. Since I was very little, I have been fortunate enough to be able to travel to many places around the world. It is amazing to have the opportunity to experience a new culture, try new foods, and see the world from a new perspective. Aside from traveling, I love to golf and draw. I find that both of these activities are very therapeutic for me in terms of relieving stress. I have been golfing for over 10 years now and it is a sport I can continue to enjoy for years to come. My interest in drawing sparked from my travels. My drawings are mostly landscape drawings of places that I have traveled to and have resonated strongly with me.
